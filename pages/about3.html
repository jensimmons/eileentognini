Past Experience

Prior to my full time professional commitment to the arts, I was Director of Marketing for an internationally acclaimed landscape architecture and urban design practice in Philadelphia. Before that, a long and successful career at the IBM Corporation provided me with the interpersonal and organizational skills that remain essential in many aspects of the art business. A two year post as VP of Sales and Marketing with international responsibility for a start up software company gave me the wonderful opportunity to travel abroad, primarily to Europe.

Currently, I serve on Collab, a group of design professionals supporting the Philadelphia Museum of Art's Contemporary and Modern Design Collection; as a member of the Art Advisory Board for the Center for Emerging Visual Artists (CFEVA); and as a member of the National Liberty Museum's Glass Now.

I'm the daughter of an artist, and mother to one. My work is dedicated to those who have, those who do, and those who will follow their dream.
