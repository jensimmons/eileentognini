Commissions

I am pleased to offer a full palette of services:

  • Begin or build a collection of fine art and interior sculpture
  • Curate an existing collection
  • Commission work for a specific interior or outdoor space
  • Commission “functional art”
  • Curate a private sculpture garden
  • Design small urban garden spaces
  • Provide proper care and maintenance of outdoor sculptural objects
  • Organize studio and gallery visits
  • Curate gallery shows and events
  • Delivery and Installation
  • Photograph and document a collection
  • Collaborate with other design professionals

Some services are offered through professional, recommended sources. Client confidentiality is strictly maintained.

Please feel free to contact me for more information.
